Half the battle to creating good design is having the right tools at hand. Figma vs Penpot? Adobe vs Affinity? v0 vs Lovable? There are too many apps to choose from!

Thankfully you don't have to.

Taken from a survey from over 50 real designers working in the industry, here's a list of the top 10 best apps to help you design better and faster in 2026.

## 1. Figma

[View Figma](https://figma.com)

### What it is

Figma is a web-based design tool that allows designers to create, prototype, and collaborate on designs in real-time. Primarily used to design user interfaces, e.g. website and mobile apps. It has become the go-to tool for many designers due to its ease of use, powerful features, and collaborative capabilities.

### My opinion?

It's no surprise that Figma is ranked number one. Out of the 50+ designers that wrote in, nearly 90% of them mentioned that Figma is a tool they use in their repetoire. Me being one of them!

With that being said, Lately, I have noticed myself using Figma less and less. If I do use it, it's more so for documenting things on the whiteboard as oppose to designing.

Creating rapid prototypes with the help of AI has made it easier and faster to come up with working prototypes to get the ball rolling. It'll be interesting to see how popular Figma will be by the end of 2026.
